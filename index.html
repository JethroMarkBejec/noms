Why Noms Exists

Noms is a cozy space where I share pastries I love and bake myself. It’s built for anyone who enjoys simple, honest baking stories. Every post is a little reminder that baking can be sweet and stress-free.

Mission

Noms aims to make baking joyful, creative, and easy to approach. Whether you’re new or experienced, there’s always something warm to discover. It’s about celebrating the process, not just the final treat.

Design Concept

The design uses soft edges, floating cards, and playful reveals. Every detail is meant to feel light and welcoming, like opening a pastry box. It turns browsing into a cozy, sensory experience.

Personal Journey

Noms began as my personal baking journal and creative outlet. It’s where I document recipes, lessons, and happy baking moments. Each entry grows with feedback, experiments, and a sprinkle of imagination.
